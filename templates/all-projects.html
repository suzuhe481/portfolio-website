NASA's Astronomy Picture Of the Day

This will let you use NASA's APOD (Astronomy Picture Of the Day) API.
You will be able to view today's APOD, a random APOD, or an APOD from a day you pick.
APOD's can be pictures, gifs, or YouTube videos.

IMDB Series Rating Chart Maker

This page let's you pick a series from IMDB and create a chart to see it's ratings.
Just paste a link from a show on IMDB, and pick a color for your chart. Note: May take longer to run if a series has many episodes.

Admin Dashboard Recreation

The purpose of this project is to recreate an admin dashboard based on an assignment on The Odin Project. There are no step by step instructions to this project, besides planning help. I am given an image, and am then told to recreate it to the best of my ability.
